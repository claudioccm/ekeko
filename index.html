  • Run's Run

    No galaxy to save, no princess to rescue. This time the enemy is yourself. Runs Run is an endless runner with a different mechanic and a plot twist. Your intestines are fighting your last meal. It was exotic and delicious but now there's a stronger force commanding you to the bathroom. You'll have a limited amount of farts to reach your goal and each fart will launch you in a frantic rush to reach the safety of the restroom.

  • About Us

    Ekeko games we believe in gameplay first and jokes second. We are 3 guys from 3 different countries that found ourselves laughing at the same lousy jokes. We decided to make short funny games that are not about spaceships or wizards. But a magical spaceship or an astronaut wizard would be cool.
